Meralco Bolts (MER)

About Meralco:

The MERALCO Bolts is a professional basketball team in the Philippine Basketball Association. The team began in 2010 after the Manila Electric Company (MERALCO) acquired the PBA franchise of the Sta. Lucia Realtors. The team is one of three PBA teams under the control of businessman Manuel V. Pangilinan - the other teams being the Talk 'N Text Tropang Texters and the NLEX Road Warriors.
