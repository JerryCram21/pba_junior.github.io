Mahindra Enforcers (KIA)

About Mahindra:

The Mahindra Enforcers first participated in the Philippine Basketball Association (PBA) Draft on August 24, 2014, two month before their first PBA season. The Enforcers (then-known as Kia Sorento) entered the league through expansion together with Blackwater Elite. Manny Pacquiao became the team's first draft choice, the 11th pick in the 2014 PBA draft.
