Barangay Ginebra San Miguel (BGSM)

About Ginebra:

The Barangay Ginebra San Miguel is a professional basketball team in the Philippine Basketball Association (PBA). It is currently the most popular PBA team and has won eight PBA titles. The team is owned by Ginebra San Miguel, Inc. A subsidiary of the San Miguel Corporation (SMC). The team is one of three PBA ball clubs currently owned by the SMC group of companies, along with the Star Hotshots and the San Miguel Beermen.
